Site's name:

Dolores Hidalgo's Chambers of Commerce

Site's purpose:

Have you ever felt overwhelmed when you travel to a new town? Always wondering: "where should I go to eat? what is the cheapest option? Where are the best souvenir stores?" and is even more stressful when you realize that there are many options and limited time? This website will help all of our foreign visitors to answer all of these questions. It'll help them save money, time and unpleasant experiences. How do you like to spend your time?

Target Audience:

My targeted audience will be English speakers who are visiting Dolores Hidalgo and want to know where is the best place to eat and buy without being scammed, which is common to happen in Mexico.

Personas:

Laura Smith decided to go to San Miguel de Allende, Mexico. She's 35 years old. Everything is so expensive in San Miguel so an acquaintance told her that everything is cheaper in Dolores Hidalgo. She has already spent a lot of money so she thinks is worth the 30-minute drive to get some souvenirs for her friends and family that are in the USA. once there she realized that, unlike San Miguel, no one speaks English and there are so many stores that claim that they have the best quality and price.

Caitlyn Smith accepted an English teaching position in San Miguel de Allende, Mexico. Before starting her new job she decided that she wanted to explore the nearby towns. She heard that Dolores Hidalgo has the best handmade ice creams, they sell cheap talavera that she can use to decorate. She does not speak Spanish, and she heard that many stores like to sell everythinga at a higher price to the foreigns. She doesn't know what store to trust and the real price of the products.
