Desing Principles

Visual Hierarchy

The Home Edit

This website follows the principle of visual hierarchy because the user is immediately attracted to see the bold text "organized in style" and the image with an example of organization. The Home Edit goal is to organize your house by following a color pattern. The website reflects the company goal by brightening the colors of their image examples by having a white background.

https://thehomeedit.com/

White Space and Clean Design

The New Yorker

The New Yorker Website has a clean design. The padding between the viewport and the text is noticeable. Also, the white space between the images and the text makes it look neat. The website uses limited colors (not counting the image colors) so the combination between the many colors of the images and the text is not overwhelming. The font size is not too small or large for the titles and text. overall, the website looks professional for using this principle of white space and clean design.

https://www.newyorker.com/

PARC Repetition

HM Cole

The rule of three helps a website form a focal point. This website is an example of the rule of three because the focal point of this suit website is the man. He is part of the middle of the grid along with the right grid. The descrption of the company is right in the middle of the grid.

https://www.hmcole.com/
